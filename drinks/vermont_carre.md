# Vermont Carr&eacute;

__Keywords__: Drinks

## Details
__Picture__: n/a

## Ingredients
1 oz. Rye  
1 oz. Cognac  
1 oz. sweet vermouth  
1/2 T. Benedictine liqueur  
Dash of maple bitters  
Dash of orange bitters  

## Directions
1. Shake with ice and serve in a chilled glass or on the rocks.
2. Garnish with an orange peel.
