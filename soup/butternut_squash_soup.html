Butternut Squash Soup

Keywords: Soup

Details

Picture: n/a

Serves: n/a

Ingredients

1 butternut squash cut in chunks
2 shallots, sliced
Rosemary
1 can chicken stock
4 slices prosciutto
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Sauté sliced shallots in olive oil, then add chunks of butternut squash, some rosemary and chicken stock or water to cover.
  2. As the soup simmers, bake strips of prosciutto until crisp.
  3. Purée the soup, swirl in some cream if you like and serve topped with crumbled prosciutto.
